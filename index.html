WHO WE ARE

In 2005, Alex and Emily founded Snapee, driven by their shared love for photography. Facing initial skepticism, they launched the platform in 2008, offering photographers a supportive online community. With innovative features and unwavering passion, Snapee quickly became a global hub for photographers, inspiring creativity in every snapshot.
